**Note:** This cover letter template contains EDITABLE sections marked with HTML comments. The personalization agent will:
1. Research the target company (website, products, mission, recent news)
2. Analyze job requirements and responsibilities
3. Extract company culture and values
4. Modify EDITABLE sections to personalize the letter
5. Keep professional tone and structure
6. Save personalized version to applications/[company_name]/cover_letter.md

**Research Guidelines for Agent:**
- Company mission and values
- Recent news or achievements (last 6 months)
- Products or services offered
- Company culture indicators
- Tech stack (for tech roles)
- Industry position and competitors
- Hiring manager name (if available on LinkedIn or company site)
